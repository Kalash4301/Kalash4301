Pink City Bazars

Johari Bajar

From gold and silver jewellery to traditional kundan jewellery you can find it all here. While you’re there you can also buy traditional Rajasthani sarees and lehengas. That’s all of Rajasthan in one market, make the most of it.

What to buy:

Kundan Jewellery

Getting There::

Rickshaws, buses, auto rickshaws, or private cabs.

Nehru Bajar

Another important shopping destination in Jaipur is Nehru Bazaar. If you’re looking at buying beautiful Rajasthani mojris then this is the place to go. There are mojris in different colours and styles and you’ll be spoilt for choice. Nehru Bazaar also has hand printed dress material and bandhani dupattas at affordable prices.

What to buy:

Mojris

Address:

Nehru Bazaar is located at MI Road, one of Jaipur’s most central locations..



Gaurav Towers

Don't be misled by the notion that just because this is a mall it will have overpriced clothes and accessories. Gaurav Towers has the best of everything all under one roof. Right from brands like Provogue, Bata, Woodland and Levis. Apart from that there are many other stores selling designer clothes, ethnic wear and accessories. This is also a great place to stock up on cosmetics. There are also many restaurants where you can sample delicious chaat, dosas and rolls.n also buy traditional Rajasthani sarees and lehengas. That’s all of Rajasthan in one market, make the most of it.

What to buy:

Handbags and cosmetics

Getting There::

Gaurav Towers is located in Malviya Nagar and you can hire an auto or a cab.
